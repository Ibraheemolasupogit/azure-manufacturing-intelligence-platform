# KPI Catalogue

Formulas are preliminary and will be formalised during the relevant analytical milestones. Milestone 1 documents the catalogue only.

| KPI | Preliminary definition or formula | Grain | Interpretation | Future sources |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Production attainment percentage | Produced quantity / planned quantity | Plant, line, shift, product | Higher indicates plan achievement. | Production events |
| Throughput | Produced units per time period | Line, machine, product | Measures production velocity. | Production events |
| Yield | Accepted quantity / produced quantity | Batch, product, line | Higher indicates more usable output. | Production events, quality checks |
| First-pass yield | Units passing without rework / total inspected units | Batch, line | Higher indicates process quality. | Quality checks |
| Scrap rate | Rejected quantity / produced quantity | Batch, product, line | Lower is better. | Production events, quality checks |
| Defect rate | Defective units / inspected units | Product, batch, line | Lower is better. | Quality checks |
| Overall equipment effectiveness | OEE = Availability x Performance x Quality | Machine, line, shift | Composite equipment effectiveness. | Production events, equipment health |
| Availability | Operating time / planned production time | Machine, line | Higher means less downtime. | Production events, equipment health |
| Performance efficiency | Ideal cycle time x total count / operating time | Machine, line | Higher means speed is closer to target. | Production events |
| Quality rate | Good count / total count | Machine, line, product | Higher means fewer quality losses. | Production events, quality checks |
| Planned downtime | Scheduled downtime duration | Machine, line, shift | Used to separate planned from unplanned loss. | Production events |
| Unplanned downtime | Unscheduled downtime duration | Machine, line, shift | Lower is better. | Production events, equipment health |
| Mean time between failures | Operating time / failure count | Machine | Higher indicates reliability. | Equipment health, production events |
| Mean time to repair | Repair duration / repair event count | Machine | Lower indicates faster recovery. | Equipment health |
| Schedule adherence | Orders completed on schedule / scheduled orders | Product, line, plant | Higher means better plan execution. | Production events, sales orders |
| Forecast accuracy | 1 - error measure such as MAPE | Product, region, period | Higher means demand estimates are closer to actuals. | Sales orders, forecasts |
| Forecast bias | Forecast quantity - actual quantity | Product, region, period | Shows systematic over- or under-forecasting. | Sales orders, forecasts |
| Inventory turnover | Cost of goods sold / average inventory value | Item, warehouse, period | Higher suggests faster inventory movement. | Inventory, sales orders |
| Days of inventory | Inventory on hand / average daily demand | Item, warehouse | Higher means more coverage. | Inventory, sales orders |
| Stockout rate | Stockout events / item-location periods | Item, warehouse | Lower is better. | Inventory |
| Service level | Fulfilled demand / requested demand | Product, customer segment | Higher means better customer fulfilment. | Sales orders, inventory |
| Safety-stock coverage | Available quantity / safety-stock quantity | Item, warehouse | Values below 1 imply risk. | Inventory |
| Supplier on-time delivery | On-time deliveries / total deliveries | Supplier, material | Higher means better delivery reliability. | Supplier performance |
| Supplier in-full delivery | Delivered quantity / ordered quantity | Supplier, material | Higher means fewer short shipments. | Supplier performance |
| On-time-in-full | Deliveries both on time and in full / total deliveries | Supplier, material | Higher means stronger supplier execution. | Supplier performance |
| Supplier defect rate | Rejected quantity / delivered quantity | Supplier, material | Lower is better. | Supplier performance, quality checks |
| Order fulfilment rate | Fulfilled quantity / ordered quantity | Product, region | Higher means better demand fulfilment. | Sales orders |
| Capacity utilisation | Actual production time / available capacity time | Plant, line | Indicates how much capacity is consumed. | Production events |
| Cycle-time variance | Actual cycle time - target cycle time | Machine, line, product | Positive values may indicate process slowdown. | Production events |

OEE is conceptual in Milestone 1 and has not been implemented.
